No Bake Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine
  • 2 tbs unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup quick cooking oats

Steps

  1. combine dry ingredients
  2. add wet ingredients
  3. mix
  4. shape
  5. cool in fridge
  6. enjoy =3
